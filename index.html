MediPlanner Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

TL;DR: MediPlanner stores all your appointment data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or access any of your information.

What MediPlanner Does

MediPlanner is a local appointment scheduling app that helps you organize medical appointments and medication reminders. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Information Storage

Local Storage Only: All data is stored exclusively on your iPhone/iPad using iOS's secure storage system.

What you can store locally:

  • Appointment dates, times, and locations
  • Doctor names and contact information
  • Medication names and reminder times
  • Personal notes about appointments

What We Don't Collect

  • No medical records or health data
  • No personal identification information
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • No cloud storage or backup

Notifications

MediPlanner uses iOS local notifications to remind you about appointments and medications. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not send any data externally.

Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS built-in security (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode)
  • Standard iOS encryption
  • No network transmission
  • No remote server access

Your Control

  • Delete Data: Remove the app to delete all stored information
  • Edit Information: Modify or delete any appointment details anytime
  • Notifications: Control notification settings in iOS Settings

Data Sharing

We do not share any data. Since all information stays on your device, there's nothing to share with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we'll post the new version here and update the "Last Updated" date.
